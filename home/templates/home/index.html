Golden Goal

Gameplay

Points

Fill out the Golden Goal tournament wallchart

  1. Begin by selecting a winning team or a draw for each of the games in the group stages. The knockout rounds will then automatically populate depending on the match results that you have set in the group stages.
  2. Continue this for the knockout rounds by selecting which team you think will progress for each of the matches right through to the final to decide your overall winner.
  3. You can save this at any time and come back to it later but once the first match has kicked off your choices will be locked in place so make sure you have it completed before then.

Note: This must be completed before the first match has kicked off.

Points are awarded as follows:

  • Group stage - 1 point for each correct match result.
  • Last 16 - 2 points for each team predicted to be in this round. Doubled for teams in the correct place.
  • Quarter final - 3 points for each team predicted to be in this round. Doubled for teams in the correct place.
  • Semi final - 4 points for each team predicted to be in this round. Doubled for teams in the correct place.
  • 3/4 play off - 5 points for each team predicted to be in this round. Doubled for teams in the correct place.
  • Final - 5 points for each team predicted to be in this round. Doubled for teams in the correct place.
  • Winner - 10 points for correctly predicting the Womens World Cup winners

Silver Goal

Gameplay

Points

Try to predict the scores of each match throughout the tournament.

Points are awarded for correctly predicting the number of goals that each team scores. A point is also awarded if the match outcome is correct.

A bonus point is awarded if you get both scores correct.

You can enter the score for each match right up until each match kicks off.

Points are awarded as follows:

  • Home team score correct - 1 point.
  • Away team score correct - 1 point.
  • Match outcome correct - 1 point.
  • Get both scores correct and get a bonus 'hat-trick' point.

This game comprises of two parts in which to score points.

  • In the Golden Goal fill out the World Cup wallchart and try to forecast each teams progress throughout the tournament, this part must be completed before the tournament kicks off.
  • In the Silver Goal daily you try to predict the scores of each match as they are played each day throughout the tournament. Scores can be entered right up until kick-off.

Golden Goal
  • Group stage - 1 point for each correct match result.
  • Last 16 - 2 points for each team predicted to be in this round. Doubled for teams in the correct place.
  • Quarter final - 3 points for each team predicted to be in this round. Doubled for teams in the correct place.
  • Semi final - 4 points for each team predicted to be in this round. Doubled for teams in the correct place.
  • 3/4 play off - 5 points for each team predicted to be in this round. Doubled for teams in the correct place.
  • Final - 5 points for each team predicted to be in this round. Doubled for teams in the correct place.
  • Winner - 10 points for correctly predicting the Womens World Cup winners
Silver Goal
  • 1 point awarded if you get the correct number of goals the home team scores.
  • 1 point awarded if you get the correct number of goals the away team scores.
  • 1 point awarded if you get the match outcome correct.
  • Score a match hat-trick, ie, get all 3 points above and you score an extra point for getting the match ball.
Overall scoring
  • Your overall score is the total number of points scored across both games.
