Golden Goal

Complete the Golden Goal tournament wallchart. Begin by selecting a result for each of the matches in the group stages. The knockout matches will automatically populate based on the results selected in each group.

Select a winner for each of the knockout matches and continue this right through to the final and predict an overall winner.

Points are awarded for each correct group match predicted correctly and for each team correctly predicted to be in each knockout round.

NOTE: This game must be completed before the tournament begins.

This game comprises of two parts, a pre-world cup wizard and a daily score predictor.

  • In the Golden Route fill out the World Cup Wizard and try to forecast each teams progress throughout the tournament, this part must be completed before the tournament kicks off.
  • In the Silver Goal daily score predictor you try to predict the scores of each match as they are played each day throughout the tournament. Scores can be entered right up until kick-off.

Golden Route
  • Group stage - 1 point awarded for each correct result
  • Knockout rounds - 1 point awarded for each team that you forecast to be in that round and an extra point awarded if they are in the path indicated by the golden route.
  • Winner - 10 points awarded if you get the correct winner of the World Cup.
Silver Goal
  • 1 point awarded if you get the correct number of goals the home team scores.
  • 1 point awarded if you get the correct number of goals the away team scores.
  • 1 point awarded if you get the match outcome correct.
  • Score a match hat-trick, ie, get all 3 points above and you score an extra point for getting the match ball.
Overall scoring
  • Your overall score is the total number of points scored across both games.
